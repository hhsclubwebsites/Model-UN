What is Model UN?

At Model UN, each student represents a country and argues in the interests of that country. This allows members to explore global problems and expose themselves to different worldviews.

How do I join?

Attend any meeting. We meet every Monday after school from 2:45 pm to 3:30 pm. No fee required.
